About Me

I grew up in eighty's and ninety's with computers (286, Amiga, 486). Naturally I was fond of gaming and went into computing and programming. During my college years, I coded in TI Basic on my TI-82 calculator.

Discovering computer possibilities, by exploring operating systems was my curiosity : BeOS BeOS (now Haiku), Plan9 With Linux, I was appealed by minimal desktop experience and windows managers : wmii, wmi, dwm, and xmonad. I loved their KISS approach. Thanks to this community, I learnt about Golang and Haskell.

I worked in compagnies as Desktop Engineer, especially into Operating System and Application Integration with tools like sysprep, imagex and Wise Package Studio. Then, I wanted to be expert on software packaging. InstEd was my preferred tooling choice. It leads me to better understand underlying windows installer model. Communication with Niel Hunt, developer of InstEd, while using his tool every day, was a unique experience.

Scripting in VBScript and PowerShell was also a great part of my job. I went a step further into automation with Windows Installer XML. Finally, I became able to automate software integration with static packaging architecture and dynamic sources absorption.

That's why, few years later, I decided to evolve into DevOps, and then Cloud DevOps. Docker, Kubernetes, IAC, Ansible..emotion I felt was like swimming for years in swimming pools and then discovering the sea. Not an expert anymore but a huge universe to discover.

Always with the same spirit of computing digging, I worked with Azure DevOps SDK with ARM Templates and PowerShell to build new kinds of services. Coding with ReactJS, TypeScript and Python was also a great pleasure.

Today I'm still in my quest of improving my programming skills in Golang, Python, Docker and Kubernetes and offering my DevOps background.

I want to become a professional software developer, I always dreamt to be.
